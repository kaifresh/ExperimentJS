(static) exports.ImageStimuliIV(iv_name, list_of_img_paths)

Automatically creates an independent variable from the paths provided.
During the experiment, a series of images (one per path) are displayed on screen. By default, these images are added to the DOM as the first child of a <div> with the ID "YOUR_IV_NAME-img-wrap" and can be styled or manipulated as required for your experiment.
